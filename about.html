Our history

Over the years there has been a food supply deficit,either from problems of infrastructure or transport.Some many even argue that lack of reputation amongst business owners largely contributes this negligence as well. There instances where buyers pays for goods in trust but ends up being swindled by there sellers.Over all,there is a great deal of mistrust among people in business. Ada miltiple ventures over the years has built strongly on foundation of integrity and strong morals and ethics amongst it workers. This has boosted our business opportunities from people from the length and breadth of the country, who are either buying and paying trust or supplying the commodities in trust. We started small from selling perishable goods on the road of the market.We did not even have shop of our own.We rode the storm of the harshest weather and some of the most malign treatment one can ever experience,to the point where we were able to rent a shop but did not have enough goods to fill. We went from gaining the trust of a few distributors to actually affording a chunk of our goods. It is been a rollercoaster journey; We encounter series of armedrobberies and property destruction by government agencies who only wanted bribe, at some point we started afresh but the good thing is we lost the money but not experience. We could pick ourselves back up as fast as possible because of the existing business relationships gathered. To Ada multiple ventures spans across the nation as a renowned business thanks to God and an unwavering resilience. We have grown from petty trading to retailing and to wholesale distributors. Our business outlets cuts across the country to give consumers the best competitive price and a value for their money. When we were well ready, we thought it well to establish our business online, ofcourse this is not an easy task as it involves a great deal of planning and resources, so that we can give customers a seemless shopping experience. We implore you to throw your shopping feedbacks and criticism as that is only how we are going to improve and serve you better,by knowing how you feel.

Giving back

We are not only about business, we also give back to the society that made us who we are today by giving you. We run promotions from time to time,please seize the opportunities. We are intent on making sure the less opportuned in our society gets quality primary and secondry education through our fully funded scholarship programmes. We priotize women in our society and have loan schemes ranging from #100,000-500,000 to business women. We also have vocational programs for widows to learn skills for free to set them up as independent women in our society. Plans are in place to see we expand our philantropy to neighbouring countries like Niger,Ghana and Cameroun, as our business is fast growing into a WestAfrican business.
